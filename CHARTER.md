# Governance Charter

## Mission

KnowledgeCivic builds public knowledge spaces that help people learn, deliberate, and participate in civic life.

## Independence

The project is non-profit in purpose and non-partisan in operation. Sponsors, donors, founders, and maintainers may support the work but may not purchase editorial conclusions.

## Editorial standard

Material must be source-aware, dated, accessible, corrigible, and proportionate to the evidence. When evidence is disputed, describe the dispute and its basis. Do not manufacture balance where the evidence is not balanced.

## Accountability

Decisions affecting content, access, safety, or contributor rights should have a written rationale. Maintainers publish periodic activity, funding, and correction summaries.

## Appeals

Contributors may request reconsideration with specific evidence or process concerns. A person who made the original decision should not be the sole appeal reviewer.
