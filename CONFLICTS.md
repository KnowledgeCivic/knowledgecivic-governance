# Conflict of Interest Policy

Contributors should disclose financial, professional, organizational, political, or personal interests that could reasonably affect a contribution.

Disclosure does not automatically disqualify a person. It allows readers and reviewers to interpret the work and lets maintainers assign an independent reviewer when needed.

Maintainers must not accept money, gifts, employment, or access in exchange for a preferred editorial outcome. Funding and material support should be recorded in the public organization repository.
